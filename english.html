Church of Maria Santissima del Monte Carmelo

The church of Maria Santissima del Monte Carmelo (commonly known as the church of the Carmine) of Taranto is one of the churches of the Borgo Umbertino of the city. Its existence dates back to 1577, but over the centuries it has been remodeled several times until it took on its current neoclassical appearance

Clock Tower

The Clock Tower of Taranto is one of the monuments of the ancient village of the city. It was built in the second half of the eighteenth century in its original form, i.e. without the advanced polygonal body and the bell tower added later.

Cathedral of San Cataldo

The cathedral of San Cataldo (or cathedral of San Cataldo) is the cathedral of the archdiocese of Taranto and is the oldest Romanesque cathedral in Puglia. Initially dedicated to Santa Maria Maddalena then to San Cataldo Vescovo.
